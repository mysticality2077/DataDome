Welcome to the DataDome! It's a site focused on being simplistic, yet working quite well. I originally was going to make this site on neocities.org, but fuck them for trying to make me waste my money to upload files.
The DataDome is a hobby website, and it will have just a ton of things in it, but I have set myself size restrictions. I want to try keeping most of it under 5 GB. I'm making sure every kilobyte is accounted for.
That's why you may hear audio or see video that is concerningly low-quality. It's just less space being taken up.

Please enjoy it! I plan to have other repos for other stuff later on, so come back to them if you want!
